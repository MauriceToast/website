About Betpuck

Betpuck is an app that allows you to bet on ice hockey with friends in private competitions!

Create or join custom leagues for French Ligue Magnus, Swiss National League, or World Cup. Customize your competition:

  • Duration: Full championship or playoffs
  • Prediction type: Match scores or winner/loser
  • Scoring method: Point scale or bookmaker quotes

Invite friends, make predictions, and compete for bragging rights! Enhance your hockey experience by challenging friends' knowledge.

Betpuck offers a secure, user-friendly platform for friendly wagers without real money!
