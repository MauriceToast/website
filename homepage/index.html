About Betpuck

Betpuck is an app that allows you to bet on ice hockey with friends in private competitions! Create or join custom leagues for French Ligue Magnus/ Swiss National League / World Cup. Customize your competition: Duration: Full championship or playoffs Prediction type: Match scores or winner/loser Scoring method: Point scale or bookmaker quotes Invite friends, make predictions, and compete for bragging rights!
