2.1.1 Creating a competition

By creating a competition, the User obtains Administrator status, and then has the choice between:

  • Creating a prediction contest based on the complete calendar of an official competition;
  • Creating a customized prediction contest (choice of competition, choice of calendar...).

Once the competition is created, the Administrator has an invitation URL (address of a website or hypertext page on the internet) and a Token (sequence of characters assigned to a competition to allow Users to join it) which they will use to invite participants to the contest.

Once the invitation is accepted and their account created, new Users can start playing.

The User has the possibility to directly join a private competition organized by an Administrator, via an invitation URL or a Token
